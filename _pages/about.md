I am an Assistant Professor in the Division of Emerging Interdisciplinary Areas at the Hong Kong Unversity of Science and Technology. Prior to that, I was an assistant professor at the Chinese Univesity of Hong Kong (Shenzhen). I received Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the [University of Notre Dame](https://www.nd.edu/), advised by Prof. [Chaoli Wang](http://sites.nd.edu/chaoli-wang/). Before that, I obtained my BS in Software Engineering and MS in Computer Software and Theory. Both are from [Xidian University](https://www.xidian.edu.cn/). My research interests lie in the general area of scientific visualization, machine learning, and human-computer interaction.
  
<b>PhD applicants:</b>  I plan to recuit 1 or 2 Ph.D. students starting in Spring/Fall 2025. If you are interested in working with me, please email to junhanvis@outlook.com after you fill out the [form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd7qmIjnJnJKUPyc0Kp0DpREb7vsH6DOMba4pkT4XpcweE5nQ/viewform?usp=sf_link). The email title should be "University-Name-PhD/RA/Visiting Student." In the email, briefly describe your motivation for wanting to work with me. Additionally, please attach your resume, academic ranking(s), and research experience (e.g., papers under review or published). Both the email and CV should be in English. If suitable, I will contact you as soon as possible. Reaching out early can help you apply for the Hong Kong government scholarship!

I will read all application emails, but I may not be able to respond to each one. If you don’t receive a response within a month, it may indicate that we are not a good fit.
